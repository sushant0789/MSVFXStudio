Our Services

MS VFX STUDIO Founded in 2014.

VFX / Stereo RotoScopy

Rotoscopy of characters or objects to produce alpha mattes for VFX Compositing, Stereo Conversion or Color Correction. The BOT can provide alpha mattes and spline files for ultimate flexibility during comp. Labeled, seperated and ready to go in any comp package.

VFX / Stereo Paint

Digital Paint-in of missing elements or paint-out of unwanted elements. By creative sleight of hand, the BOTs remove wires and markers without jitter and with proper re-graining. It takes a BOT touch to lovingly restore handfuls of frames or entire sections of reels from scratch and dust specs.

Stereo Conversion

This may be applied to any elements of a VFX shot for layer-logical stereo conversion, including live-action and computer graphic elements.

2D Animation

2D animation figures are created or edited on the computer using 2D bitmap graphics or created and edited using 2D vector graphics. This includes automated computerized versions of traditional animation techniques, interpolated morphing, onion skinning and interpolated rotoscoping.
